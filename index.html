The App

Built for people who drive for work

Real estate agents, freelancers, contractors — anyone who needs to track miles for taxes.

Automatic tracking

Tap once. Track everything.

Hold the button to start a trip and MILEWAY logs every mile in the background — even while you use other apps. Your GPS route is recorded precisely so you have a verifiable log come tax time.

  • Background GPS tracking — no need to keep the app open
  • Auto-stop after you've been parked for a few minutes
  • See today's miles and dollar value on the home screen
Trip history

Every trip logged with a map.

Each trip shows the start and end address, total miles, and a minimap of your route. Filter by Business, Personal, or Unclassified. Export a clean CSV at tax time with one tap.

  • Automatic address lookup for start and end points
  • Business / Personal / Unclassified categories
  • Monthly summary view to see totals at a glance
Full route replay

See exactly where you went.

The Location screen shows your full GPS track on a map. Use the slider to select the exact segment you want to save as a trip — useful when you started tracking early or forgot to stop.

  • Full-resolution GPS route drawn on map
  • Trim trip start and end with a range slider
  • 898-point precision on every route
Odometer & voice notes

Log it fast. Even while driving.

Record a quick voice note at any time — MILEWAY transcribes it automatically. Log your odometer reading to keep a complete mileage record that satisfies IRS documentation requirements.

  • Voice notes with automatic transcription
  • Odometer log with date and time stamps
  • All data stored locally — never uploaded to any server
